Neologia is a domain-specific language for building dictionaries and lexicons, and is well-suited for the customizability required by constructed language dictionaries. Write your words and their definitions in a simple syntax, customize the order of your alphabet, and use sections to specify automatically linked synonyms, antonyms, derivations, and much more! Neologia also comes with several automatic checks enabled to help prevent common mistakes that occur when building lexicons, and generates standard Markdown output.
